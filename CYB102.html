Design a secure Small Office Home Office network implementation including VLANs, IP addressing scheme, and security measures. Please ensure all documentation is submitted directly to the Moodle assignment folder.

Final Exam Study Guide

The final exam is comprehensive. Please prioritize reviewing:

  • Subnetting: IPv4 binary conversion and CIDR notation.
  • Routing: Static routes vs. Dynamic protocols.
  • VLANs: Tagging, Trunks, and Inter-VLAN routing.
  • Security: Firewall types, VPNs, and common attacks.

Exam closes Dec 15 at 8:00 AM.
